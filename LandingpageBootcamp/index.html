All you need to know about the Bootcamp

Is this Bootcamp suitable for non-technical professionals?
Absolutely. In fact, 70% of our previous cohorts were from non-tech backgrounds (Marketing, HR, Finance, and Operations). This program focuses on "Strategic AI Usage"—how to leverage existing tools to solve business problems—rather than writing code. If you can use a browser, you can master these AI tools.
What specific outcomes can I expect in my daily work?
You will be able to automate repetitive document work, generate high-fidelity visual assets, build internal data dashboards, and set up autonomous agents to handle lead research and outreach. Most participants report saving 10-15 hours per week by the end of Week 4.
Will I receive a formal certification?
Yes. You will receive an industry-recognized Certificate of Completion from the AI Innovators Club. This certification validates your mastery of Prompt Engineering, Workflow Automation, and No-Code Build frameworks, making it a powerful addition to your LinkedIn profile.
Are there corporate group discounts or GST invoicing?
Yes, we provide GST-compliant invoices for corporate reimbursements. For group enrollments (5+ participants from the same organization), please reach out to us for specialized team-training rates and executive-focused modules.
What happens if I miss a live weekend session?
Every session is recorded in high definition and uploaded to your personal dashboard within 2 hours of completion. You will have lifetime access to these recordings, along with the source materials, tool-lists, and prompt libraries.
Will I need to pay for AI tool subscriptions?
We prioritize free-usage tiers for all 30+ tools taught in the program. While we demonstrate advanced features of paid versions (like ChatGPT Plus or Midjourney), you can achieve 90% of the course outcomes using the free tiers we've vetted and selected for this curriculum.
